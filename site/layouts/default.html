What is this?

Every day, we look at every tweet sent by official U.S. government Twitter accounts two days previously. We score them all based on retweets, favorites, and the size of their audience and present the results here. We use USA.gov’s Social Media Registry API to gather the accounts we look at.

This site is in early beta, so keep checking back for updates.
